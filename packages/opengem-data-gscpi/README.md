# opengem-data-gscpi

🧪 alpha

NY Fed Global Supply Chain Pressure Index (GSCPI) adapter.

GSCPI is a monthly composite of 27 supply-chain stress indicators (container
shipping, airfreight, PMI delivery times, etc.) maintained by the Federal
Reserve Bank of New York. Free CSV download; no auth.

Per R06 §3, GSCPI is a primary information-surface input for OPENGEM's L3
nowcast and Stratfor-grade scenarios.

## Single series

- `WORLD.NYFED.GSCPI.supply_chain_pressure.M`

## Source

CSV at https://www.newyorkfed.org/research/policy/gscpi (under "Latest Data")
