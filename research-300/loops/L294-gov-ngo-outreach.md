# L294 — Government / NGO outreach plan

**Loop**: 294 / 300
**Phase**: 6 — Synthesis + launch
**Date**: 2026-06-06

---

## The thesis

Government + NGO outreach is **slow-burn institutional credibility**, not a discovery driver. The right institutions taking OPENGEM seriously over the long arc is what makes the Y5 "cited next to WEO" milestone (L010) plausible. None of this work pays off in months; all of it pays off over years.

The OPENGEM stance: we approach these institutions as a *technical peer who has built something they should evaluate*, not as a vendor seeking a contract. The conversation is "review this", not "buy this."

## What NOT to do

- **No solicited consulting engagements with government agencies during Y0-Y2.** Editorial dependency risk.
- **No exclusive data-licensing agreements with sovereign agencies.** Killing the open-substrate promise.
- **No "sponsored research" arrangements where the sponsor influences research direction.** Editorial integrity.

## The single channel that matters most

**OPENGEM appearing on the WEO / OECD EO / NY Fed Nowcast / Federal Reserve SEP comparison list as a tracked alternative forecaster** within 24 months. This is the load-bearing institutional milestone.

How we get there:
1. Build the open ledger (already done in research-300).
2. Run a verifiable backtest with public scoring (L184-L195).
3. Submit the methodology + replication to the relevant IMF/OECD/Fed researcher (L292).
4. Wait for one of them to cite us in a footnote of a calibration report.
5. The institutional recognition compounds.
